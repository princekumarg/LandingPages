ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

Top Racer Ranges

Golf for all ages for your friends and family With easy access from Central and South East London, Sidcup Family Golf offers a 54-bay floodlit driving range and a premier golf academy with five golf professionals.

Advanture Golf

Golf lessons for all ages and abilities are available as group classes or individual tuition. Our training facilities also include an interactive video coaching studio to really analyse and improve your game. Focus on your short game in our Short Game Practice area, then enjoy a light bite in our Golf Cafe or visit our golf superstore.
